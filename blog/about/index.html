What I write about

Five pillars, on purpose.

The blog has five pillars and nothing else. Each one is a lens on the operating reality I’ve lived — money, growth, team, operations, and the founder calls behind all of it.

  1. 01 Money decisions Forecasting that doesn’t lie. Pricing that doesn’t apologize. Cash, capital, and the line items most owners avoid until they can’t.
  2. 02 Growth traps The deals that look big and aren’t. The marketing that buys vanity. The growth that breaks the company that earns it.
  3. 03 Team reality Hiring without hope. Delegation without dumping. The actual mechanics of getting work out of other humans.
  4. 04 Operations Cadence, process, decisions. The systems that make a business answerable to itself.
  5. 05 Founder decisions The calls only the operator can make — about energy, ego, focus, and the costs of being in charge.
